How it works

Two parts, one clean click path.

1

Install the desktop app

Create URL-to-profile rules in the Companion App and manage browsers, devices, licenses, imports, exports, and sync.

2

Link browser profiles

Add the TabFlow extension to each browser profile you use, then link those profiles to the app.

3

Click normally

When a URL belongs elsewhere, TabFlow opens it in the mapped profile automatically and lets you override when needed.

Extension + app

The app decides. The extension moves.

The Companion App is the source of truth for rules, profiles, and licensing. The browser extension watches navigation and handles the redirect experience. If the app is not running, your browser simply behaves normally.

FAQ

Practical details before you switch it on.

Do I need both the app and the extension?

Yes. The desktop app manages mappings and profiles; the extension handles redirection inside each browser profile.

What happens if TabFlow is not running?

Nothing breaks. The extension stays idle and your browsing works normally until the app is available again.

Does TabFlow require an account?

No traditional account is required. Purchases are activated with the license information from your receipt.

Can I sync mappings?

Sync is optional and separate from licensing. TabFlow is local-first and can use providers you connect.
